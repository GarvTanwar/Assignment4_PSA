Ages 9-10

Building routine

Children at this age can take more responsibility for small household checks. They can start remembering energy-saving actions without needing a reminder every time.

What they can do

Kids can check switches, unplug chargers, turn off screens, and help with bedtime or leaving-home energy routines.

Parent approach

Give children a small role in the family routine, such as being responsible for checking one room before leaving the house.

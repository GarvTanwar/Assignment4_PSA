Topic 04

Simple habits parents can teach kids

Children do not need complicated lessons to start saving energy. They need clear examples, steady reminders, and parents who make responsible choices part of normal home life.

Action checklist

  • Ask children to switch off lights when they leave a room.
  • Teach them to unplug chargers after devices are charged.
  • Use natural light during the day before turning on lamps.
  • Set a family routine for turning off screens before bedtime.
  • Explain the power bill in simple terms so kids understand waste has a cost.

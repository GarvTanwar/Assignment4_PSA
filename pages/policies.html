Website Policies

These notes explain how Powering Tomorrow content may be used and how this website handles basic privacy and accessibility expectations.

Terms of Use

This website is for educational campaign purposes. Visitors should use the information as general guidance and check official energy advice for personal, school, or household decisions.

Privacy Policy

Powering Tomorrow does not ask visitors to create accounts or submit sensitive personal information. If someone contacts the campaign, their details should only be used to respond to that message.

Accessibility

The site is designed with clear headings, readable contrast, keyboard navigation, and alternative text for meaningful images. Feedback about accessibility issues can be sent through the contact page.

Copyright & Usage

Original campaign text and visuals belong to Powering Tomorrow unless a source is listed on the references page. Referenced facts and third-party sources should be credited when reused.

Ages 15-16

Leading by example

Teenagers can manage their own energy habits and influence the rest of the household. They are old enough to take ownership of devices, screens, and shared spaces.

What they can lead

Teens can avoid leaving chargers plugged in, manage gaming and screen time responsibly, and remind younger siblings through their own example.

Parent approach

Give teenagers independence and responsibility. Treat energy use as part of maturity, not just another household rule.

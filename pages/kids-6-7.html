Ages 6-7

Learning the basics

Children at this age need simple habits that are easy to remember and repeat. Parents can focus on short reminders and visible routines around the home.

What they can learn

Kids can practise switching off lights, closing doors when heating or cooling is on, and unplugging chargers with help from parents.

Parent approach

Use calm reminders, simple language, and praise when children remember. The goal is to make saving energy feel like a normal family routine.
